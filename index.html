Nutrition Monitoring Application

For my Final Year Project, I'm building a Nutrition Monitoring Application using ReactNative as my framework as it would be easier to simulate app in both Android and iOS.

Some of the key features of this project include;
- Calorie Counting
- Measurement of water intake
- Recommended meal plan
- Daily food diary logging
- Simple cooking recipe instructions
- Simple exercise plan

All this is to ensure that the end-user would be able to hit their daily required calories to maintain and stay fit based on how actively they exercise.
